Guidelines

1. Location bound - Projects supported by Fairbed (Amsterdam) must be for the benefit of local cooperative community of Fairbed Amsterdam

2. Legality – Projects proposed must not be breaking any local rulings that are set by local municipality or any of the overarching national or EU laws

3. Value – The overall business value / social benefits of the proposal must ideally exceed the actual costs of the projects

4. Short-term to Medium-term – Projects duration must not exceed 18 months to complete so that the local community will be able to benefit from the projects in the short to medium term

5. Budget allocation – The overall projects must not exceed € 1000 in costs. Only projects that need funding have to be proposed in the Fairbed platform for support

6. Feasibility – Only projects that are feasible (after full Coop Staff reviewing process) and backed up by full documentation will be approved

7. Approvals obtained – If the projects are to concern property owners (municipality, land owners, etc), prior approvals must be obtained from these stakeholders before proposal submission
