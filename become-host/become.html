Proposing Community Projects

How are the service fees redistributed?

For each booking, a fraction of your service fees are redistributed to support yours and others' projects funds. A remaining part is spent to cover running the cooperative platform.

Supporting Projects

As a host you can decide which project(s) you wish to support with the money that is in your project fund.
